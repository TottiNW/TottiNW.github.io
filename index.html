Player bio

Unity/C# generalist who likes turning ideas into playable mechanics.

Most of my projects are roguelikes and dungeon-crawlers. Not by accident, as that is where procedural systems, combat, exploration and atmosphere all come together. I like the under-the-hood bits: a generator that makes a new level every run, encounters that feel fair but unpredictable, and enemies that behave the way the design promises.

Systems are not the whole job though. The work I am most proud of is also about meaning and feel: light, space, atmosphere, story, and the sense of being somewhere with a reason to keep going. I want to make inclusive, atmospheric and system-driven games that are not only fun to play, but leave the player with something to remember.
